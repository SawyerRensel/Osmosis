## Card with Nested Lists

```osmosis
id: list-nested

What are the three branches of the US government and their roles?
***
- **Legislative** (Congress)
  - Senate (100 members)
  - House of Representatives (435 members)
  - Makes laws
- **Executive** (President)
  - Enforces laws
  - Commander-in-chief
  - Signs or vetoes bills
- **Judicial** (Supreme Court)
  - Interprets laws
  - Reviews constitutionality
  - 9 justices, lifetime appointment
```

## Card with a Task List

```osmosis
id: checklist-debug

What are the steps to debug a failing unit test?
***
- [ ] Read the error message carefully
- [ ] Check the test expectations
- [ ] Add `console.log` or breakpoints
- [ ] Run the test in isolation
- [ ] Check for shared state between tests
- [ ] Review recent changes with `git diff`
```
