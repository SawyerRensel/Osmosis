## Key Integrative Concepts

- Hypertension is the leading risk factor for heart failure, stroke, and CKD
- Heart failure treatment targets the neurohormonal axis (RAAS, SNS) activated by reduced cardiac output
- Valvular disease can cause both pressure overload (stenosis) and volume overload (regurgitation)
- ECG interpretation is essential for diagnosing arrhythmias, ischemia, and structural changes
- Pharmacology bridges diagnosis and treatment — drug choice depends on the underlying pathophysiology
