# Cardiology Rotation — Study Plan

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Morning (7-8 AM) | Afternoon (5-6 PM) | Evening (8-9 PM) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | Anatomy review | Osmosis flashcard review | Practice ECGs |
| **Tuesday** | Physiology deep-dive | Osmosis flashcard review | Read guidelines |
| **Wednesday** | Pharmacology | Osmosis flashcard review | Case studies |
| **Thursday** | Pathology | Osmosis flashcard review | Board questions |
| **Friday** | Integration / review | Osmosis flashcard review | Practice ECGs |
| **Saturday** | Weak topic review | — | Board questions |
| **Sunday** | Rest / light review | — | Plan next week |

## Week 1: Foundations

- [x] Review heart anatomy (chambers, walls, valves)
- [x] Trace blood flow pathway (pulmonary + systemic)
- [x] Learn coronary artery territories
- [x] Complete all Cardiovascular System flashcards
- [x] Complete all Blood Flow Pathway flashcards
- [ ] Practice identifying heart sounds (S1, S2, S3, S4)
- [ ] Review fetal circulation shunts

## Week 2: Diagnostics & Pathology

- [ ] Master systematic ECG interpretation approach
- [ ] Learn all ECG intervals and normal values
- [ ] Study heart failure classification (NYHA, EF-based)
- [ ] Understand systolic vs diastolic HF differences
- [ ] Complete ECG Interpretation flashcards
- [ ] Complete Heart Failure flashcards
- [ ] Practice 20 ECG strips from the ECG library

## Week 3: Pharmacology & Hypertension

- [ ] Learn all major drug classes (beta-blockers, ACEi, CCBs, diuretics)
- [ ] Memorize drug suffixes and prototypes
- [ ] Study hypertension staging and treatment algorithm
- [ ] Understand secondary causes of hypertension
- [ ] Complete Cardiac Pharmacology flashcards
- [ ] Complete Hypertension flashcards
- [ ] Work through 30 pharmacology board questions

## Week 4: Valvular Disease & Integration

- [ ] Study each valve lesion (stenosis vs regurgitation)
- [ ] Memorize murmur characteristics and locations
- [ ] Learn physical exam maneuvers that affect murmurs
- [ ] Complete Valvular Disease flashcards
- [ ] Review all flashcards using Osmosis spaced repetition
- [ ] Take a full-length cardiology practice exam
- [ ] Review weak areas identified by Osmosis analytics

## Resources

- **Primary**: Osmosis mind maps and flashcards (this vault)
- **Supplemental**: Pathoma Chapter 9, First Aid Cardiovascular section
- **ECG Practice**: Dr. Smith's ECG Blog, Life in the Fast Lane
- **Board Prep**: UWorld Step 2 cardiology questions

## Goals for This Rotation

1. Score ≥ 80% on the end-of-rotation cardiology shelf exam
2. Be able to independently interpret 12-lead ECGs
3. Know first-line treatments for common cardiac conditions
4. Master the cardiac physical exam (auscultation, JVP, peripheral pulses)
5. Have all Osmosis flashcards at "mastered" stability before the exam
